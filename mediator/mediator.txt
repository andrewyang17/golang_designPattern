What is Mediator?
- Mediator reduces coupling between components of a program
  by making them communicate indirectly, through a special mediator object.
- A component that facilitates communication between other components without them
  necessarily being aware of each other or having direct (reference) access to each other.
- Media engages in bidirectional communication with its connected components.
- Mediator has methods the components can call,
  components have methods the mediator can call.
